1) You are given a chart, where chart candle shows the price, red line is the moving average(50), and x-axis of chart is on hourly-basis covering 1~2 days range.
2) Analyze the pattern with given chart, along with the current investment status and orderbook, and predict whether price would go up or down after 3 hours.
